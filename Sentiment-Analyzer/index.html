Week 1 — AAA Game Pitch: Spec Ops (Remake)

Training program deliverable: build contextual research and a pitch-ready summary. This page shows the rationale and results.

Project context

Week 1 — Training program. Task: create a pitch to present to an investment panel for a AAA game remake or new IP.

To support the pitch, I built a sentiment analyzer and ran it over public chatter for the company’s dormant titles. Reddit was used as the primary social signal because of its large, engaged gaming communities and thread-level discussion depth.

Methodology (brief)

  • Collected Reddit posts and comments mentioning the company’s dormant titles (time-bounded crawl).
  • Preprocessed text (cleaning, tokenization, stop-word removal).
  • Built a sentiment analyzer tuned for gaming discussions (lexicon + fine-tuned classifier).
  • Scored titles by aggregate positive sentiment, discussion volume, and trend velocity.

Key finding

The analysis showed that Spec Ops generated the most chatter and strongest positive sentiment on Reddit around the idea of a remake. This made it the prime candidate for the AAA pitch.

Top title: Spec Ops
Why: highest positive signal + sustained volume + community interest in a remake

Pitch summary (elevator)

Opportunity: Remake Spec Ops as a modern, single-player cinematic AAA experience with optional co-op and live-service adjacent features. Community analysis indicates strong brand recognition and active demand that can be converted into preorders and early-engagement marketing.

Why now? Nostalgia-driven remakes have high conversion when combined with clear modernizations (combat, AI, storytelling). Reddit sentiment shows both older fans and younger players discussing the franchise and requesting a return.

Value props to emphasize to investors

  • Built-in audience and demonstrable demand via social signals.
  • Lower concept-risk compared with brand-new IP; faster path to monetization.
  • Potential for franchise reactivation across DLC, remasters, and media tie-ins.
